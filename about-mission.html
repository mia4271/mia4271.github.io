COMFORT MAPLE AGING INNOVATIONS INC.



Comfort Maple Aging Innovations Inc. (CMAI) is a Canadian Consulting Firm specializing in Senior Healthcare Management consultation engaging with relevant Canadian Health authorities , Agencies, Suppliers, and Community organizations. We are the FIRST consulting firm that provides Canadian senior care expertise and industry consulting services in China. In Collaboration with the China Association of Social Welfare and Senior Services (CASWSS), CMAI is devoted to providing professional management consulting services on leading edge research, rehabilitation, and resources for the Senior Care delivery in China. CMAI is committed to ensuring a mutually rewarding experience in improving health Human Resources and while maintaining a people centred care approach.
